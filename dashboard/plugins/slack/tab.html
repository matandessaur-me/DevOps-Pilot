Slack Bridge

Connect your Slack workspace to read channels, reply to threads, and post messages -- all without leaving DevOps Pilot.

  1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and click Create New App
  2. Choose From scratch, name it, and pick your workspace
  3. Go to OAuth & Permissions
  4. Under Bot Token Scopes, add:
    channels:history channels:read chat:write groups:history groups:read im:history im:read mpim:history mpim:read users:read reactions:write
  5. Click Install to Workspace and authorize
  6. Copy the Bot User OAuth Token (starts with xoxb-)
  7. In DevOps Pilot, go to Settings > Plugins > Slack Bridge and paste the token
